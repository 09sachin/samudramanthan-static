Quant Solar

Quant solar is a clean-tech company that aims to develop innovative and smart technologies for a sustainable future. Quant solar offer a safe and reliable solution that has been tested on international standards and guidelines of operability and safety. Our goal is to reduce risk across the solar value chain while delivering more reliable, dependable and cost-effective solutions for our customers.
