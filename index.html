About Us

About IIT Kharagpur

Dedicated to the service of the Nation”, the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur is a public engineering institution established by the government of India in 1951. It is the first of the IITs to be established, and is recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India and has won the tag of Institutes of Eminence (IoE).

About the department

Being the first of its kind, the department was established in 1952. In its 68 years of allegiance, it has made significant contributions to the development of shipbuilding and shipping industries in the nation including the Indian Navy, Indian Coast Guard, DRDO and other allied organizations. The department and its alumni have strategic links with universities, shipyards and organizations across the globe.

Legacy of samudramanthan

Samudramanthan ’19 witnessed active participation of students from all corners of India. The hard work and dedication complemented with the innovation and creativity in our learners made it a grand success. With all our exhilaration and enthusiasm, we take immense pleasure to present our 12th edition with the mission to bestow the students with a myriad of intellectually challenging events.
