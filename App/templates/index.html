About IIT Kharagpur

"Dedicated to the service of the Nation‚Äù, the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur is a public engineering institution and the first among all IITs established by the government of India in 1951. It is recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India and has won the prestigious tag of Institute of Eminence (IoE).

About the department

The Department of Ocean Engineering and Naval Architecture was established in 1952, the first of its kind in the whole nation. In it's 70 years of service to the nation, it has made significant contributions to shipbuilding and shipping industries, including the Indian Navy, Indian Coast Guard, DRDO and other allied organizations. The department and its alumni have strategic links with universities, shipyards and organizations across the globe.

Samudramanthan 2021
