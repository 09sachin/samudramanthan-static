Sponsors 2021

Title Sponsor

Quant Solar

Quant solar is a clean-tech company that aims to develop innovative and smart technologies for a sustainable future. Floating Solar Solution obviates the need for large tracts of land by installing solar panels on lakes, ponds, reservoirs, lagoons etc. which are available in abundance in most countries.
Quant solar offer a safe and reliable solution that has been tested on international standards and guidelines of operability and safety. Our goal is to reduce risk across the solar value chain while delivering more reliable, dependable and cost-effective solutions for our customers.

Major Sponsor

Bouyancy Consultants

Buoyancy Consultants approach to offshore & ship design is through providing quality engineering services for new builds, retrofits, Brownfield and Greenfield projects.

Events Sponsor

GRSE

From being a small workshop for repairing vessels in 1884 to building the first warship of Independant India, GRSE has delivered 107 warships to the Indian Navy, Indian Coast Guard and also to the Mauritius and Seychelles Coast Guards since the take over from the Indian Government. It is the biggest Defence Shipyard in India and has diversified its field.
We consistently invest in human technology, research and analysis to simplify solutions for complex engineering requirements

SHOFT

SHOFT is an ISO 9001 -2015 DNV Certified shipyard, holding Industrial License for construction of Warships in India established in 1987 is now a privately held shipbuilding company promoted and run by Naval Architects. The Head Office is at Thane (Mumbai) and the shipyard is located at Bharuch, on the coast of river Narmada near Dahej in Bharuch, Gujarat, the state with the longest coast line in India. SHOFT consolidated assets and nurtured a great work force, evolving into a full - fledged medium sized shipyard with supporting infrastructure. It has a build capacity which allows simultaneous building of 9 – 12 vessels of varying sizes upto 150m length and launching weight upto 6000 tonnes. SHOFT provides Infrastructural support for India’s coastal security fleet and contributes to Defence R&D.
