AquaMOD

Designing a ship from scratch is a time-intensive process, so why not use standard designs to achieve the optimal vessel design? Aquamod is a unique remodeling event where the goal is to improve upon an existing design. Participants are challenged to improve the hydrodynamics, internal plans, and overall performance of the vessel while adhering to strict metrics. As a sponsor, this event presents you with the opportunity to optimize existing designs by having dedicated teams from around the world work on it using sophisticated software such as Maxsurf, Solidworks, and Ansys.

Energex

Energex is a case study event that challenges participants to work on analyzing and proposing ocean-energy solutions for the Indian coastline. Participants will work on diverse fields in the renewable energy sector, including the use of hydrokinetic, tidal and wave energy keeping in line with the “blue bloom”. As renewable energy becomes a reality, industries are rapidly moving to harness technology and innovations in this sector. Energex is an excellent opportunity for your firm to implement the research our participants conduct.

−

Innotect

Innotect serves as a platform for future strategists and consultants to develop and implement novel ideas in maritime logistics. Ports and container terminals are in dire need of modernization, and we challenge participants to develop robust algorithms to efficiently use port and vessel space. Teams also have the opportunity to design optimized berthing and bay arrangement plans. The results of their extensive research is available to your firm, and we are certain that these unique perspectives have immense potential!

−

Oceania

The Oceania quiz provides a platform for the nation’s sharpest minds to compete and showcase their knowledge. The event tasks participants with taking on thought-provoking challenges that progressively become difficult over different rounds. Battling through unique challenges, Oceania’s winners are crowned based on their reaction times and in-depth understanding of the naval architecture and marine engineering fields. For those from an engineering discipline, this is the perfect opportunity to pit their minds against the top budding engineers of India.

−

Oceania Quiz

Oceania truly has a legacy to live up to. With unprecedented participation in the last few years, it has received much praise from our participants for being challenging, yet informative. For anyone with an interest in anything remotely marine, we have you covered with this interesting quiz that goes right from bottom of the sea to the engine room of a cruise ship. Rest assured, your grey cells will be charged up with our special brand of questions!

−

BeyondQ

BeyondQ, now in its 2nd edition, is an amazing opportunity for those with a penchant for solving puzzles, codes, and crosswords. It challenges the participants on the aspects of time management and intellect, pushing them to think outside the box to solve demanding tasks. It is targeted at introducing non-marine students to the wonderful opportunities this field has. Participants take it out over multiple rounds with increasing difficulty to ultimately reign champion in another innovative event from Samudramanthan!

−

Paper Prep

As budding researchers and engineers, new innovations and ideas should have the proper platforms for discussion, idea-exchanges, and positive feedback. Paper prep is a conducive platform where industry experts and IIT Kharagpur professors evaluate and ideate on diverse topics studied by our participants. And who knows, the next innovation you may be looking to implement may just be waiting to be discovered in this event!

−

Capture the Art

Capture the Art is a unique event and a chance for participants to showcase their creative flair. Tasked with creating digital or hand-drawn artwork on diverse themes related to the marine & maritime industries, this fun, quirky event is the perfect opportunity for budding artists to explore their interests. After all, even engineers can be creative and fun! Potential topics range from important occasions in these sectors, to various areas of research that your firm may be engaged in.
