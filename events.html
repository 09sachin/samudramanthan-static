Oceanic Maze

Ships and vessels often have to sail on various types of water bodies- from open oceans and seas, to narrow constricted channels. Simply ensuring that the ship floats and moves is not sufficient and one must ensure that the vessel is able to accurately steer away from obstacles, and towards stretches of open water. To test their maneuvering prowess, participants are challenged to construct a boat that is able to navigate through a carefully crafted maze without triggering the collision detection mechanism.

Clean The Pool

Cleaning the waste generated from oil spills and garbage dumps from our water bodies is no mean feat. Collecting immense amounts of waste that are scattered over wide stretches of water is challenging. We intend to support the ocean waste management systems through technology and innovation by challenging the participants to design boats and waste collection mechanisms that can successfully clean up the target area within the specified time.

TechnoArch

The basic job of a Naval Architect is to design the boats and ships ranging from the small ones to the massive tankers. This event requires the participants to design a particular boat according to the problem statement and present the design details during the presentation.

BoatExpo

Why only study with books when you can build your own models ? BoatExpo invites participants taking part in the Marine Robotics events to showcase their boats, their thinking behind the design and their journey in the preparation of the model. This event will consist of a presentation regarding the above mentioned topics.

Case Study

Analyzing historic events and past cases addressing peculiar circumstances in production, planning and execution processes from different viewpoints can assist in solving real world problems in a fast and efficient manner. For those with an aptitude for scrutinizing real-world cases, Samudramanthan presents an exciting opportunity that challenges participants to study and analyze critical events and come up with smart solutions.

Paper Presentation

Innotect

Any industry in order to grow requires the ideas to be nurtured from the grass-root level. Open ideas lead to innovations and further development of existing technologies. This event invites papers on a specific topic in the maritime industry to enhance the shipping and logistics.
