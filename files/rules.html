Rules

1. Each team can have 3 members.
2. Any number of teams per institute are allowed.
3. Team members can be from different colleges.
4. There will be two stages − first, the participants are required to submit their research paper online, second is the presentation.

Specifications:

Round 1 :

1. The last date of submission of the abstract is 01 March 2020. No more entries will be entertained after that.
2. You must submit your abstract at www.samudramanthan.in/events
3. The paper should be submitted in PDF format, (Times New Roman font, size 12, with single spacing, font color black in MS word).

Round 2 :

1. Teams will have to give their presentation in Samudramanthan 2020.
2. The participants must now furnish a document attested by their guide/ mentor clearly stating that the concerned participant(s) have done original work relevant to the submitted paper. The paper must bear the signature and the seal of the Professor/ Head of Department.
3. The teams will get 10 minutes to present their idea before the jury. They will then have 1 minute to wind up. This will be followed by a question and answer session of 5 minutes duration. Marks will be deducted for every minute exceeded after 11 minutes.

Judging Criteria :

Judgment will be at Judges' discretion and marks will be deducted for exceeding the time limit.
